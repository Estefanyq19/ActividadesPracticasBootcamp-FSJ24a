Omelette Recipe

Classic Omelette

Prep

5 mins

Cook

5 mins

Total

10 mins


Ingredients

4 large eggs
room temperature

1/4 cup whole milk

1/4 teaspoon kosher salt

2 tablespoons unsalted butter


Instructions

Beat the eggs and milk until light and frothy.

Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add the buter.

Pour in the eggs. Let them set for a few seconds.
